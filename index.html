The FPT University

About

Being a newly established university located in the business sector, FPT University has a profound understanding of market demands and is highly responsive to advancements in science and technology. From this understanding, the university has developed and implemented a distinctive training program, emphasizing individual development linked with diverse experiences and simultaneously providing updated knowledge from internationally accredited training programs along with professional skills and the capacity for lifelong self-learning to students, contributing to the development of a knowledge-based economy and the creation of a happy society.

More info

FPT University believes that each individual has the ability to “sculpt themselves” and create their own success. The school advocates comprehensive personal development for students through academic, cultural, artistic, and training activities towards the goal of harmonious intellectual, physical, and spiritual development for learners.
